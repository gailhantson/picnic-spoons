Studio


Alongside writing and research, I also build thoughtful websites for writers, artists, independent projects, and small creative businesses.

I’m especially interested in helping people create spaces on the internet that feel personal, durable, and easy to live with over time–blogs, digital gardens, portfolios, libraries, newsletters, archives, and simple websites designed around clarity rather than noise.

Much of the modern web feels crowded, disposable, and optimized for short attention spans.

I’m drawn instead to slower and more human-centered approaches to publishing online: sites built for reading, reflection, learning, and long-term growth.

Services may include:

  • Personal blogs and websites
  • Writer and artist portfolios
  • Hugo and static-site setup
  • Website organization and structure
  • Copywriting and editorial support
  • Digital libraries and resource pages
  • Simple custom styling and design
  • Long-term website planning for evolving projects

I especially enjoy helping people clarify what they are actually trying to build–not just visually, but conceptually. Many good projects fail because their structure never reflects the deeper purpose behind them.

If you’re working on a thoughtful project and need help shaping or building it, feel free to get in touch.
